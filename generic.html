Bobby Lussier

Bobby resides in Burlington, Vermont, and has been spotted in the back corners of coffee shops and on google maps street view outside numerous post offices. When he isn’t slinging mail or sipping bean juice, Bobby is probably thinking about environmental justice or trying (unsuccessfully) to network with farmers.

Kyle Murphy

Kyle Murphy (not to be confused with Secret Kyle) is the Special Projects Coordinator for Non Book Club as well as our resident graphic designer and Dungeon Master. Without him, the podcast would have absolutely zero visual appeal. Residing in scenic New Jersey, Kyle brings an air of whimsy and adventure to the pod, especially when he doesn’t listen to the album but shows up to discuss anyway. His favorite musical muses are ABBA, Weezer and Reel Big Fish and he always prefers mushrooms. Send him some cool pictures of dragons and he’s sure to be your lifelong pal. Lastly, Kyle is a Scorpio...so take that as you will.

Nina Tripp

Nina Tripp is a beautiful gem of a human being. More importantly, Nina is a genuine punk. She is perhaps the most dedicated person in Non-Book Club. Though a New Jersey native, Nina now lives in Amsterdam, often staying up until 3am to discuss which song inspired tattoo she’s getting next. One could call her a global icon. When she isn’t dog walking, playing board games, or recovering from a bike related injury, you can find Nina creating incredible art including the bios featured on our page! Remember to check out @saltwaterpaintings for more
